Pan-Tilt 3D Scanner

https://github.com/ljiang09/3D-Scanner

Assumptions:

    - Build and program a rudimentary 3D scanner using a pan/tilt mechanism

    - Scan an object of known, well-defined geometry

    - Visualize the output from your scanner




Contributions:

    - Programmed servo movement using Arduino

    - Wired Arduino to servos and IR sensor

    - Assisted in parsing and visualizing scanned data


The completed mechanism scanning a cardboard cutout of the letter "L".
The scanned data visualized in a 3D space. The 'L' can be seen at the bottom of the plot.
